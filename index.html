About Yazer Fish Shop

Started by our grandfather near Pollachi Railway Station, he was among the first wholesale fish merchants in Pollachi, sourcing fish from Rameswaram by train in thermocol boxes.

After market losses, the business continued as a trusted retail fish shop. Today the third generation continues the legacy with hygienic packing, fresh seafood, fast local delivery and digital branding.
